## ✉️ PART 2: Email Scheduling Sequence Workflow Logic

To seamlessly schedule the pre-drafted emails according to the client's strict constraints (**Every 10-12 days, completely omitting weekends**), follow this automation mapping in GHL Workflows:

### ⚙️ The Smart Delay System Architecture

Do not use generic wait blocks. In GHL, we deploy a conditional time-window queue to satisfy both rules effortlessly.

[Contact Trigger: Enters Active Clean Contacts List]
│
▼
[Action: Send Email 1]
│
▼
[Action: Smart Wait Step]
⏱️ Time Delay: 11 Days
🚫 Advanced Window Settings:
Checked: Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri
UNCHECKED: Saturday, Sunday
│
▼
[Action: Send Email 2]
│
▼
[Action: Smart Wait Step] (11 Days - Weekends Blocked)
│
▼
[Action: Send Email 3]...

### 🛠️ Step-by-Step GHL Configuration

1. **Initiate Campaign:** Navigate to **Automation ➔ Workflows ➔ Create New Workflow** (Start from Scratch). Name it: `Sly Nerds - Long-Term Email Nurture Sequence`.
2. **Define Entry Criteria:** Add a workflow trigger targeting contacts entering the saved `Active Clean Contacts` Smart List.
3. **Draft Selection:** Map your first sequence block to load the corresponding email draft directly from the pre-written Claude artifact.
4. **Program the Interval (The 11-Day Sweet Spot):** Below the email node, insert a **Wait** action. Set delay mode to `Time Delay` and value to `11 Days`.
5. **Enforce Blackout Dates:** Open the **Advanced Window** toggle within the wait action block. Check only weekdays (**Monday through Friday**). Leave Saturday and Sunday completely unchecked.  
   * *Why this works:* GHL counts 11 days. If day 11 lands on a Saturday, the automation pauses execution and seamlessly releases the contact to receive the email on Monday morning.
6. **Set Global Execution Rules:** Navigate to the workflow's **Settings** tab at the top of the interface:
   * **Allow Re-entry:** Keep Disabled.
   * **Sender Profile:** Manually assign name as `Sly Nerds` and fill the verified outbound domain account email.
   * **Safe Distribution Windows:** Limit outbound execution strictly to office hours (e.g., `08:00 AM - 05:00 PM`) based on the targeted region's timezone to ensure zero middle-of-the-night pings.
7. **Publish:** Save and toggle the master switch from **Draft** to **Publish**.
